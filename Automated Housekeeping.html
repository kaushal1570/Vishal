Automated Housekeeping:-

Automated housekeeping is a modern technology-based solution that automates various aspects of cleaning and maintenance in residential and commercial properties. It utilizes advanced sensors, robotics, and artificial intelligence (AI) to create a more efficient and cost-effective cleaning process. The automated housekeeping process begins with sensors that detect the areas that need cleaning. These sensors can detect areas that are dirty, dusty, or cluttered and alert the cleaning system to focus on those areas. Once the system receives the signal, it sends a robotic cleaner to the area to clean it. Robotic cleaners are programmed to perform various cleaning tasks, such as vacuuming, mopping, and dusting. They use sensors to navigate around the property, avoiding obstacles and adjusting to changes in the environment. They can also perform more complicated cleaning tasks, such as deep cleaning carpets and upholstery.
